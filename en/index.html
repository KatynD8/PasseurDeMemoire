Why make a filmed biography?

To pass on your memories through images, to share the sound of your voice with your loved ones and future generations. This is possible and easy with a 30 to 60-minute biographical film including photos or videos. From an interview, you can authentically share your memories, your values, your story. It is important to preserve your memory and strengthen intergenerational ties. Have you ever told your love story? Your career path, passions, commitments? Many people regret not having filmed memories of their parents or grandparents, especially their voices‚Ä¶ This is now possible thanks to a professional who helps you transmit your memories and emotions through a filmed biography.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it difficult to speak in front of the camera?

Not at all, the interview is guided with kindness, like a natural conversation.

How long does the filming last?

Generally, filming lasts between 1h30 and 3h. It depends on the person, the subjects covered, and the richness of their story.

Can we add photos?

Absolutely, you can provide photos, films or documents (paper or digital) that I integrate into the editing. This enriches the story and makes it even more lively.

Are the videos confidential?

Yes. The videos are intended only for your family and loved ones. No content is shared without your explicit consent.

How long before receiving the video?

Depending on the complexity of the editing, it usually takes about 3 to 4 weeks after filming.

In what format is the film delivered?

The video is sent via a downloadable link and provided on a USB stick in a universal format playable on computer or TV.

Can a video biography be given as a gift?

Of course! Many families choose to give this project to a parent or grandparent.
