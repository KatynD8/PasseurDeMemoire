Why make a filmed biography?

Passing on your memories through images, sharing the sound of your voice, for your loved ones and future generations, is possible with a 30 to 60-minute biographical film including photos or videos. Based on an interview, you can authentically share your memories, values, and legacy. It is important to preserve your memory and strengthen intergenerational bonds. Have you ever shared the story of your love encounter? Your professional journey, your passions, your commitments? Many people regret not having filmed memories of their parents or grandparents. This is now possible thanks to a professional who helps you transmit your emotions and memories through a film.

How does it work?

I meet you for the first time to introduce myself, get to know you, and prepare the interview. Together we discuss your life, your achievements, your parents and loved ones. We prepare the topics and questions you want to address. We decide on the story you wish to tell and the key moments you want to share. During filming, I capture your life story through an on-camera interview. It is an intimate and privileged moment with you that you will share with your family. The process is always kind and fully respects the wishes you expressed during the preliminary meeting. After the interview, I take care of editing your video biography according to the vision we established for an authentic and sincere narrative. You can also choose the music or photos you want to include in the video. Your video biography will be delivered a few weeks later as a downloadable link and on a USB stick so you can watch your film on a computer or television.

Who am I?

A passionate person. I had a first professional life in France in the world of retail and training. After arriving in Israel, I studied at the University of Jerusalem to become a tour guide. I am passionate about passing on the history of Israel and the Jewish people and for several years I have been a TV columnist for Tandem TV. I have always been driven by the desire to pass on and share. Memory Keeper is the logical continuation of this mission, with a human approach, attentive listening, and a strong sense of connection. I am driven by an authentic curiosity filled with kindness. Filming, pacing, telling a story: I love turning a story into a shared emotion.
