What is a filmed biography?

Have you ever wanted to tell your life story or that of a loved one? To share your childhood in your country of origin, your profession, or your immigration journey? Because you are not just a parent or grandparent ‚Äî your life is full of rich and moving experiences.

A filmed biography is a legacy that connects generations.

Why make one?

Your story deserves to be shared with your loved ones. Preserving your memory strengthens intergenerational bonds.

Thanks to a 30- to 60-minute biographical film, you can pass on your memories in images, enhanced with your own photos or videos.

Share your values, passions and anecdotes with your own voice in an authentic way: have you ever told the story of your first love? Your career path? The life of your parents?

How do I guide you?

Meeting and preparation

Before the interview, we begin with an initial meeting (2 to 4 hours, in the location that suits you best) to exchange ideas and create the right conditions for your testimony. We define themes and questions, important parts of your story, people to mention, as well as documents or photos to include.

Filming in a chosen setting

I capture your life story through an interview, at home, outdoors or in a meaningful place (half a day). It is an intimate and precious moment to later share with your loved ones. My approach is always caring and respectful of your wishes.

Editing and delivery

Once the interview is done, I edit your biography with your archives (documents, photos, etc.) to create an authentic narrative. The video will be delivered within a few weeks as a downloadable link or on a USB stick, ready to watch on any device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it difficult to speak in front of a camera?

Not at all, the interview is guided with kindness, like a natural conversation.

How long does filming last?

Usually between 1h30 and 3h, depending on the person and richness of the story.

Can we add photos?

Absolutely, you can provide photos, films or documents to integrate in the editing.

Are the videos confidential?

Yes, they are only intended for your family and relatives, unless you explicitly agree otherwise.

When will I receive the video?

On average within 3 to 4 weeks after filming, depending on the complexity of the editing.

In what format is the film delivered?

The video is provided as a downloadable link and on a USB stick in a universal format.

Can the biographies be filmed in Israel?

I travel for the first meeting and interview within 100 km of Tel Aviv.

Can a video biography be given as a gift?

Of course! Many families choose to offer this project to a parent or grandparent.
