Why make a filmed biography?

Passing on your memories through images, sharing the sound of your voice with your loved ones and future generations is possible with a 30 to 60-minute biographical film including photos or videos. Based on an interview, you can authentically transmit your memories, values, and personal story. It is important to preserve your memory and strengthen intergenerational bonds. Have you already told the story of your love encounter? Your career path, your passions, your commitments? Many people say they regret not having filmed memories of their parents or grandparents. It is possible thanks to a professional who helps you transmit your emotions and memories through a film.

How does it work?

I meet you for the first time to introduce myself, get to know you, and prepare the interview. Together, we talk about your life, your achievements, your parents, and your loved ones. We prepare the themes and questions you want to address. We decide on the story you wish to tell and the important moments you want to share. During filming, I capture the story of your life through a face-to-camera interview. It is an intimate and special moment with you that you will share with your loved ones. The process is always caring and fully respects the wishes you expressed during the preliminary interview. Afterward, I edit your video biography according to the vision we established, for an authentic and sincere story. You can also choose music or photos you would like to include in the video. Your video biography is delivered a few weeks later as a downloadable link as well as on a USB stick so you can watch your film on a computer or television.

Who am I?

A passionate person. I had a first professional life in France in the world of retail and training. When I arrived in Israel, I went back to university in Jerusalem to become a tour guide. I am passionate about sharing the history of Israel and the Jewish people, and for several years, I have been a TV columnist for Tandem TV. I have always been driven by the desire to pass on and share. Memory Keeper is the logical continuation of this will to transmit, with a human approach, active listening, and always an excellent sense of contact. I am animated by genuine curiosity, full of kindness. Filming, pacing, storytelling: I love transforming a story into a shared emotion.
