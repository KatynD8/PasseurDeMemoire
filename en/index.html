Why make a filmed biography?

To pass on your memories through images, to share the sound of your voice, for your loved ones and future generations. This is possible and easy with a 30 to 60-minute biographical film including photos or videos. From an interview, you can authentically share your memories, values, and story. It is important to preserve your memory and strengthen intergenerational bonds. Have you ever told your love story? Your professional journey, your passions, your commitments? Many people regret not having filmed memories of their parents or grandparents. How does it work?

Meeting and preparation
I first meet with you to introduce myself, get to know you, and prepare the interview. Together, we discuss your life, your parents, and your loved ones. We prepare the topics and questions you wish to address. We decide on the story you want to tell and the important moments you want to share. We review the photos or documents you wish to include in your testimony.
Filming in a chosen setting
The filming can take place at your home or outdoors in a place dear to you. During filming, I capture the story of your life through an on-camera interview. It is an intimate and special moment with you that you will share with your loved ones. The process is always caring and fully respects the wishes you expressed during the initial meeting.
Editing and delivery of the film
After the interview, I edit your video biography according to the vision we established, for an authentic and sincere story. You may also choose music or photos to insert in the video. Your video biography will be delivered a few weeks later as a downloadable link or on a USB stick so you can watch it on a computer or television.

Who am I?

A memory keeper and a passionate storyteller. I had a first career in France in the retail and training industry. After moving to Israel, I studied at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem to become a tour guide. I am passionate about transmitting the history of Israel and the Jewish world, and for several years I have been a TV columnist on related topics. I have always been driven by the desire to transmit and share. Being a memory keeper is the logical continuation of this will to transmit with a human approach, attentive listening, and an excellent sense of contact. I am guided by authentic curiosity, full of kindness. Filming, pacing, storytelling: I love turning a story into shared emotion.
