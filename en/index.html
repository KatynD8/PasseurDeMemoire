Why make one?

Your story deserves to be shared with your family. Preserving your memory strengthens intergenerational bonds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it hard to speak on camera?

No, the interview is guided with care, like a natural conversation.

How long does filming take?

Usually between 1.5 and 3 hours, depending on the richness of the story.

Can we add photos?

Yes, you can provide photos, films, or documents that I will integrate into the editing.

Are the videos confidential?

Yes, they are only for your family and close circle, unless you agree otherwise.

When will we receive the video?

On average within 3 to 4 weeks after filming, depending on editing complexity.

In what format will we receive the film?

It will be delivered via download link and USB stick in a universal format.

Can biographies be filmed in Israel?

Yes, I travel within 100 km of Tel Aviv for meetings and interviews.

Can we offer a filmed biography as a gift?

Of course! Many families choose to offer this as a gift to a parent or grandparent.
