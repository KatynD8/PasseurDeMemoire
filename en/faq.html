Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the filming last?

In general, a filming session lasts between 1.5 and 3 hours. It depends on the person, the subjects and themes discussed, and the richness of their story.

Can photos be added?

Absolutely, you can provide photos, films, or documents (paper or digital) that I integrate into the editing. This enriches the story and makes it even more vivid.

Are the videos confidential?

Yes. The videos are intended only for your family and relatives. No content is shared without your explicit consent.

How long does it take to receive the video?

Depending on the complexity of the editing, it usually takes 3 to 4 weeks after filming.

In what format is the film delivered?

The video is sent via a downloadable link and provided on a USB stick in a universal format readable on a computer or television.

Can a video biography be offered as a gift?

Of course! Many families choose to offer this project to a parent or grandparent.
