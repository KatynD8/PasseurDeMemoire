Here you will find answers to the most common questions about our video biography creation process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the filming last?

On average, a session lasts between 1.5 and 3 hours. It depends on the person, the topics covered, and the richness of their story.

Can we add photos?

Absolutely. You can provide photos, films, or documents (paper or digital) that I integrate into the editing. This enriches the story and makes it even more vivid.

Are the videos confidential?

Yes. Videos are intended only for your family and close ones. Nothing is shared without your explicit consent.

How long before we receive the video?

Depending on the complexity of the editing, it usually takes 3 to 4 weeks after the filming.

In what format is the film delivered?

The video is sent via a downloadable link and provided on a USB stick in a universal format, viewable on computer or television.

Can we offer a video biography as a gift?

Of course! Many families choose to gift this project to a parent or grandparent.
