Frequently Asked Questions

Is it difficult to speak in front of the camera?

Not at all, the interview is guided with kindness, like a natural conversation.

How long does the filming last?

Generally, a filming session lasts between 1.5 and 3 hours. It depends on the person, the topics covered, and the richness of their story.

Can photos be added?

Absolutely, you can provide photos, films, or documents (paper or digital) that I integrate into the editing. It enriches the story and makes it even more vivid.

Are the videos confidential?

Yes. The videos are intended only for your family and loved ones. No content is shared without your explicit consent.

How long does it take to receive the video?

Depending on the complexity of the editing, it usually takes about 3 to 4 weeks after filming.

In what format is the film delivered?

The video is delivered via a downloadable link and on a USB stick in a universal format readable on computer or television.

Can a video biography be offered as a gift?

Of course! Many families choose to offer this project to a parent or grandparent.
